A Little About Me

Equipped with a BFA (Bachelor of Fine Arts) degree in graphic design, I've collaborated with clients such as First Hawaiian Bank, Pepsi, and ESPN during my time in Hawaii.

Having relocated to Chicago in 2017, I excelled as a Sr. Graphic Designer within a commercial real estate company. I spearheaded the conceptualization and implementation of compelling visual narratives across a spectrum of marketing materials and developed unique branding for over 30 commercial properties across Chicago and its suburbs.

Currently, I'm a Sr. Art Director at a performance creative agency.
